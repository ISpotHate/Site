Welcome to iSpotHate

Hate Speech Detection

What does this do?

iSpotHate is a project which uses machine learning to prevent hate speech on several social media platforms to include a safer space for everyone.

There are many projects out there that predict hate speech, but there are some glaring issues. Some examples are - apps that are limited and have a low accuracy value to determine certain meanings.
Some are marketed to wrong audience and are just simply difficult to use!

Instead, our app is preemptive, which means it works to mitigate social injustice before it happens! It is also marketed towards children/teenagers who are just beginning to use social media and are in need of guidance.
There is also a possibility of harmful sites being accessed on accident/on purpose and parental control (for a younger age group)

PROS:
1) In addition, the Chrome extension makes it very applicable for anyone to use easily.
2) Automated and able to call REST APIs easily.
3) Includes highly accurate results/F1 scores.
4) Involves ensembling both models and processes and is easy to correct validation loss or misinterpretations.

Why is this project important?


Historically, lesbians, gay men, bisexual individuals, and transgender people have not been understood and not accepted as “normal” on the human spectrum. Instead, they were stereotyped as deviants. Gladly, this worldview of the past is now changing for the better!

Sexual orientation and gender identities are integral parts aspects of ourselves and should never lead to discrimination or abuse.
Hate speech may sprout feelings of anxiety and insecurity which members of vulnerable targeted groups may experience and that leads to a negative mindset clouded with depression.
Detection of hate speech is important because it can help prevent these harmful effects.
In recent times, social media has become a hotbed for hate speech. Hate speech on social media can have harmful effects on society.
